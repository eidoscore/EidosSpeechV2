Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

eidosSpeech ("we", "our", or "the Service") respects your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it. By using our service, you agree to these practices.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: If you register, we collect your email address and a hashed password.

Usage Data: We log API requests, including IP addresses, timestamps, character counts, and voice selections. This is used for rate limiting, abuse prevention, and analytics.

Text Content: The text you submit for TTS generation is processed immediately and temporarily cached to improve performance. We do not store your text content long-term or use it to train AI models.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the TTS service and generate audio.
  • To enforce rate limits and prevent abuse.
  • To send transactional emails (verification, password reset).
  • To improve service performance and reliability.

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party providers:

  • Microsoft Azure: Our TTS engine is powered by Microsoft Edge TTS. Text content is sent to Microsoft's servers for processing.
  • eidosStack: Our authentication and infrastructure are part of the eidosStack ecosystem.

5. Data Retention

We retain account information as long as your account is active. Usage logs are retained for a limited period for security auditing. You may request account deletion at any time.

6. Cookies and Storage

We use local storage and session cookies solely for authentication and maintaining your login session. We do not use third-party tracking cookies for advertising.

7. Security

We use industry-standard encryption (TLS/SSL) for data in transit and bcrypt for password hashing. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be announced on our website.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via eidosStack.
